If the site is down please contact the Milwaukee Police Department IT Help Desk at MPDHELP@milwaukee.gov.

How to modify the list of calls
To change the Police District...
Select the MPD Police District from Police District drop-down list. For more information, click here.
To update the data...
Click the Refresh button to update the list of calls.
The system is updated every 5 minutes.
